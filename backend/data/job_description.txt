About the job
We’re seeking a Customer Platform Solution Architect to lead the strategic design and evolution of Suncorp’s Customer Data Platforms. This role is central to how we deliver personalisation at scale, drive data integrity, and enable seamless integration across marketing, sales and service journeys.

You’ll guide solution design across a range of modern integration and governance tools, ensuring we have the right platform and data architecture to support current needs and future vision.

What You’ll Do

Architect the end-to-end customer and marketing data platform across CRM, MDM, Salesforce, APIs and event-based systems
Lead design of customer master data solutions – including survivorship, identity resolution, and golden record logic
Demonstrate expertise in architecting data solutions using platforms such as Redshift or Databricks, with a strong command of advanced data modelling techniques
Enable data stewardship, consent management, and PII compliance through scalable architecture
Support real-time integration between systems like Kafka, MuleSoft, and other enterprise applications
Define platform patterns and guardrails aligned to strategic data models
Create architecture views using ArchiMate methodology
Collaborate closely with marketing, engineering, risk, and governance stakeholders
Coach and influence teams to enhance architecture maturity and promote customer-centric thinking

What You’ll Bring

Deep solution architecture experience in Customer & Marketing Data Platforms, CRM or enterprise integration
Strong knowledge of customer data models, hierarchical relationships, and segmentation logic
Experience with event-driven design (e.g., Kafka), data contracts, and lineage tracking
Comfort navigating complexity and leading cross-functional discussions with marketing, compliance, business and technology teams
Experience in Data & Application Architecture including AWS services, Databricks and Integration technologies
Familiarity with security, consent frameworks, PII handling, and governance best practices
Clear communication skills and the emotional intelligence to influence across business and technology
